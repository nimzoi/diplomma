# Aggregated Formal and Editorial Requirements for Diploma Theses

## 1. General and Formal Requirements

A diploma thesis (Bachelor’s or Master’s) must be prepared in accordance with institutional standards and submitted in a complete, formally correct, and editorially consistent form.

The thesis must be **hardbound** in a **subdued, dark color**, such as:

* navy blue
* black
* burgundy
* dark green

The thesis may be printed **single-sided or double-sided**, at the author’s discretion.

The **cover** must clearly state the **type of thesis**, e.g.:

* Bachelor’s thesis
* Master’s thesis

## 2. Language Requirements

* Theses may be written in Polish or another approved language.
* If the thesis is written in a language other than Polish, it must include:

  * an **additional title page in Polish**,
  * a **Polish abstract**,
  * **Polish keywords**.

## 3. Mandatory Structure of the Thesis

Each diploma thesis must include the following elements in the specified order:

1. **Title page**

   * Prepared strictly according to the official institutional template.

2. **Abstract**

   * Maximum length: **1,000 characters (including spaces)**.

3. **Keywords**

   * Minimum: **3 keywords**, maximum: **5 keywords**.

4. **Table of contents**

   * Automatically generated from properly formatted headings.

5. **List of abbreviations** (if applicable).

6. **Introduction**.

7. **Main chapters and subchapters**

   * Chapters and subchapters must be **numbered**.
   * No periods after chapter or subchapter titles.

   **Heading format:**

   * Chapter title: Times New Roman, **14 pt, bold**
   * Subchapter title: Times New Roman, **12 pt, bold**

8. **Conclusion / Summary**.

9. **List of tables** (if tables are used).

10. **List of figures / illustrations** (if used).

11. **Bibliography**.

12. **Additional materials** (e.g. filmography, appendices, list of illustrations), if applicable.

## 4. Length Requirements (Text Volume)

* **Bachelor’s thesis**:

  * Minimum **45,000 characters (including spaces)**
  * Equivalent to approx. **25 standard pages**

* **Master’s thesis**:

  * Minimum **72,000 characters (including spaces)**
  * Equivalent to approx. **40 standard pages**

## 5. Text Formatting and Layout

* Font: **Times New Roman**
* Main text size: **12 pt**
* Line spacing: **1.5**
* Margins: **2.5 cm on all sides**
* Text alignment: **justified**
* Page numbers: **mandatory**

### Footnotes:

* Font size: **10 pt**
* All references must appear as **footnotes**, not in-text citations.
* Punctuation marks must be placed **after the footnote number**.

## 6. Citations and References

* All sources must be cited using **footnotes**.
* Repeated references must use standardized forms such as:

  * *Ibidem*
  * *op. cit.*
* Paraphrased references should be marked with *cf.* / *see* equivalents.
* Internet sources must include the **full URL and access date**.

## 7. Bibliography

* The bibliography must be placed **at the end of the thesis**.
* Sources must be ordered **alphabetically by authors’ surnames**.
* If multiple works by the same author are listed, they must be ordered alphabetically by title.
* Only sources **actually cited (or references in the content) in the thesis** may appear in the bibliography.
* The text must **include highlighted fragments** of references to bibliographic items.

## 8. Tables, Figures, and Illustrations

* Each table, chart, or figure must have:

  * a **title placed above**,
  * a **source placed below**.
* No period is placed after titles.
* A period **is required after the source**.
* All visual materials must be included in the **list of tables / figures**.
* If illustrations appear in the text or appendix, a **list of illustrations with sources** is mandatory.

## 9. Academic Integrity

* All quotations, paraphrases, illustrations, tables, and figures must be properly referenced.
* Failure to cite sources constitutes a breach of academic integrity.

# Self-Check Checklist for Students ✅

Use this checklist before submitting your thesis:

### Formal Requirements

* ⬜ Hardbound thesis in an approved dark color
* ⬜ Correct thesis type stated on the cover

### Structure

* ⬜ Official title page used
* ⬜ Abstract (max. 1,000 characters) included
* ⬜ 3–5 keywords included
* ⬜ Automatically generated table of contents
* ⬜ Introduction and conclusion included
* ⬜ All mandatory lists included (tables, figures, abbreviations if needed)
* ⬜ Bibliography included at the end

### Length

* ⬜ Bachelor’s thesis: minimum 45,000 characters
* ⬜ Master’s thesis: minimum 72,000 characters

### Formatting

* ⬜ Times New Roman, 12 pt, line spacing 1.5
* ⬜ Margins set to 2.5 cm
* ⬜ Page numbers included
* ⬜ Headings correctly formatted and numbered

### Citations and References

* ⬜ All sources cited in footnotes
* ⬜ Correct use of *Ibidem* and *op. cit.*
* ⬜ Internet sources include access dates
* ⬜ Bibliography ordered alphabetically

### Tables and Figures

* ⬜ Titles above tables/figures
* ⬜ Sources below tables/figures
* ⬜ Lists of tables and figures included

### Language (if applicable)

* ⬜ Polish title page, abstract, and keywords added (for non-Polish theses)

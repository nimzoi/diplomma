# **📝 Evaluation Task: Assessing the Completeness and Quality of the Thesis**

---

## **🔹 Part 1: Evaluation Questions (Yes/No)**

Mark **YES** or **NO**.

1. Does the thesis include a clearly defined **background, motivation, aims, and scope** according to the Introduction chapter guidelines?
2. Does the literature review provide a **critical analysis**, rather than only descriptive summarizing of sources?
3. Are all **data types** used in the project properly described, documented, and organized (including metadata and naming conventions)?
4. Does the thesis contain a complete **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** with statistics, visualizations, and inspection of anomalies and missing values?
5. Are **standardization and normalization** steps clearly explained and justified?
6. Are all ML models described using a consistent structure: problem → data representation → architecture → hyperparameters → training → evaluation → interpretation?
7. Does the thesis contain an IT system architecture description covering layers, integration, security, and deployment aspects?
8. Does the thesis include a **Results and discussion** chapter (Task 07) with clearly presented outcomes, tables/figures, interpretation, and limitations?
9. Does the thesis include a summary, conclusions, and recommendations for future work according to the final chapter guidelines?

---

## **🔹 Part 2: Short Answer Questions**

10. Identify one major strength of the thesis in terms of structure or adherence to methodological guidelines.
11. Identify one area that requires improvement to make the thesis more methodologically complete.
12. Assess whether the ML model descriptions are detailed enough to enable replication. Explain briefly.
13. Does the presented IT system architecture meet criteria of scalability, security, and integration? Provide a short justification.
14. Which data preprocessing stage (EDA, standardization, normalization) is executed best, and which is weakest? Explain briefly.
15. Comment on the alignment between the thesis objectives and the results of the ML models. Were the objectives achieved?

---

## **🔹 Part 3: Practical Task**

16. Based on the model evaluation guidelines, **propose one additional plot, table, or metric** that should be included to improve the assessment of the ML models.
    Include a brief explanation of why this addition would be valuable.

---

## **🔹 Part 4: Final Overall Evaluation (One Sentence)**

17. Write **one sentence** summarizing your overall assessment of the technical and academic quality of the entire thesis.

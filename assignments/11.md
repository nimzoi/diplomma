## **4. Data Exploration, Standardization, and Normalization**

(From preprocessing guidelines – )

A multi-stage preprocessing pipeline is described:

### **Data Exploration (EDA)**

The work investigates dataset quality by searching for missing values, outliers, correlations, distribution patterns, and inconsistencies across all modalities. EDA uses statistical methods and visual analysis to understand the structure and issues within the data.

### **Standardization**

All datasets are converted into consistent formats, measurement units, resolutions, encodings, and structural patterns. Examples include unifying date formats, resizing images, resampling audio, and harmonizing text encoding.

### **Normalization**

Variable scales are harmonized using min–max scaling, z-score standardization, L2 normalization, or modality-specific techniques (pixel scaling, audio amplitude normalization, etc.).
These steps ensure comparability and prepare the data for modeling.
